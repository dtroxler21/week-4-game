Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target score. You lose the game by going over the target score.

The value of each crystal will be hidden from you until you click on it. Each time you play, the game will change the values of the crystals and the target score.
